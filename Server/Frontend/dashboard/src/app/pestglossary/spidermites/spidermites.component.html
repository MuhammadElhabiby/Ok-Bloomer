Spider Mites Genus: Tetranychus urticae Size: tiny - less than 1 mm long

Infestation:

  • Look for spider-like webbing
  • Take a tissue and wipe gently on the underside leaves
  • If it comes back with steaks of Spider Mite blood - you have mites

How to get rid of spider mites:

  • Spray with water gentely
  • Use neem oil (natural extract) - longe lasting solution!

Make your own homemade remedies / insectial soap:

  • Mix 1 tablespoon into 1 quart of water
