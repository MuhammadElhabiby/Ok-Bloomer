White flies Genus: Hemipterans Size: tiny - around 1 mm long

Infestation:

  • Look like small white moths (easy to spot!)

How to get rid of spider mites:

  • Use beneficial bugs as ladybugy, green lacewings or delphatus catalinea

Make your own homemade remedies / insectial soap:

  • Mix 1 tablespoon into 1 quart of water
